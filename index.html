The Heritage

1948

Ferry Porsche builds his sports car dream: the 356 "No. 1" Roadster. The birth of the Porsche brand.

1963

The legendary Porsche 911 is presented at the IAA in Frankfurt. Designed by F.A. Porsche, it becomes an icon.

1986

The Porsche 959 demonstrates what is technologically feasible, winning the Paris-Dakar Rally.

2019

Porsche enters a new era with the Taycan, the first purely electric Porsche sports car.
